Help with Stories

Create a page that combines text and other media

Stories are long-form multimedia texts. They provide a way to author complex documents that weave together text with other MediaKron content.

When to Use a Story

Stories are quite flexible and can be used for text only essays or for multimedia documents that include a variety of MediaKron content.

Stories are particularly useful when:

  • you have a lot of text
  • you want content to flow down the page
  • you want to weave together blocks of text and other media

Formatting Text

Basic formatting options appear in the formatting bar at the top of the page.

Adding Links

To add a link to an external website or other content in the site, click the " Link" button at the top of the page

Embedding Other Content

To embed other MediaKron items, click return/enter and the Add button will appear to in the right margin.

Layout Options

To customize the appearance of embedded content, click on an item and an options bar will appear. This allows you to control how the width of the content and how the text flows around the item.
