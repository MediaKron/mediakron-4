Help with Timelines

Display content chronologically

A timeline is a collection that organizes content according to date or temporal relationship.

There are two types of timelines:

  • A traditional timeline based on dates with years.
  • A generic timeline without years, used for general representations of temporal relationships such as in the lunar cycle or a timeline of pregnancy.

All content types can be added to a slideshow.
