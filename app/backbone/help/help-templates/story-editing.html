Editing Tips

Formatting Text

Basic formatting options appear in the formatting bar at the top of the page.

Adding Links

To add a link to an external website or other content in the site, click the " Link" button at the top of the page

Embedding Other Content

To embed other MediaKron items, click return/enter and the Add button will appear to in the right margin.

Line Spacing and Paragraphs

Pressing the return/enter key will add a paragraph with a line of spacing after it.

If you want a line to follow right after another without any space, hold the shift key down while hitting return/enter to add a line break.
