Help with Videos

Embed streaming video

A video is the basic item type for video-based media. Video items require links to video files already hosted elsewhere.

Supported Media Types:

  • Google Drive: A video hosted in Google Drive Instructions for Google Drive videos
  • YouTube: A video hosted at http://www.youtube.com
  • Vimeo: A video hosted at http://www.vimeo.com
  • MP4: upload an mp4 video file (under 100MB only)
  • BC Streaming Audio: audio hosted specifically on the Boston University streaming media server.
  • MP4: a link to a MP4 file hosted on the open web (no login required)
  • Streaming Video (RTMP): general format for video hosted on a streaming server
Find out more about Videos on the MediaKron Help site
