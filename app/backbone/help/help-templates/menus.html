Help with Menus

Menus provide the custom global navigation for a MediaKron site and offer an overview of how your project is organized. You can create menus that link either to site content or to external URLs.

Tips:

  • Keep your the titles of content you add as menus short. Otherwise, they may not display properly in the top navigation bar.
  • Try to keep the number menu items to fewer than five. More may not fit in the top navigation bar and will not provide an effective overview of the site organization. You can always group content into subfolders to create deeper levels of navigation if necessary.
Find out more about Menus on the MediaKron Help site
