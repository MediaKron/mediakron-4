Help withVideo Clips

If you would like to have a video start and stop at a particular time, you can add time codes in the format hh:mm:ss (hours:minutes:seconds).

Supported Formats

The following video formats are supported for creating clips: 

  • Youtube
  • Vimeo
  • BC streaming videos 

These videos are not currently supported:

  • Google Drive
  • .MP4
  • RMTP

How to Create a Video Clip

1. Find the exact times from the video you could like to clip. 

For example, if you want a video to start 5 minutes and 20 seconds and end at one hour and 10 minutes, you would translate this to 00:05:20 and 01:05:00 respectively. 

Remember to include the first 00 if the start time is less than an hour and the last 00 if the timecode does not have seconds. 

2.Add the time codes into the fields in the video add/edit form.
